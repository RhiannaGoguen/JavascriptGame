Story and Background


Welcome to my Javascript project!

On this page, you can play a very basic game that I've called "Get Out, Phillipswee!" where you take control of Phillipswee, the Raven familiar of my Dungeons and Dragons character, Deeno. Phillipswee has fallen into a hole, and it's up to you to get him out!

Javascript and HTML are used in this project, with a lot of help from the tutorials from W3Schools.


This is a photo of the real Phillipswee on the left, because it's fun to have a 'real' familiar when I'm playing DnD! The second photo is a commission I got of both Phillipswee, and my character, Deeno. It was drawn by Hijinxx Art.

Instructions


The objective of this game is to guide Phillipswee out of the hole. To do this he needs to fly out of it and get back outside, avoiding a few obstacles along the way that seem to be sticking out of the sides of the hole.

If you collide with an obstacle, the game will restart, so be careful!
To descend, just don't hit the fly upwards button.
To restart the game, just refresh the page.

Keyboard Controls

W: Fly Upwards
A: Move Left
D: Move Right
